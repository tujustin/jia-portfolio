Ahh , New York City. The city that never sleeps, the financial and fashion capital of the world. The one and only. Welcome to my food page! Some of these places are more iconic and some of them are my personal favorite! I hope you get a chance to try them all!

Jia's Top Picks

Katz’s Deli

Address: 205 E Houston St, New York, NY 10002

This is Katz’s Deli, if you haven’t heard about them then you’ve probably lived under a rock. it’s arguably just as good if not better than Zinger man’s (in Ann arbor) Do you see that meat to bread ratio? 500/10!!

Absolute Bagels

Address: 2788 Broadway New York, NY 10025

You can’t mess with the king of non-bagel breakfast sandwiches: The bacon egg n cheese. P.S. your pronounce it bacon egg n cheese on a roll. Always order it with salt,pepper, and ketchup from your local bodega 5/5!!

Prince Street Pizza

Address: 205 E Houston St, New York, NY 10002

This is prince Street Pizza. My favorite Pizzeria in all of NYC. If your pepperoni slice doesn’ have this much pepperoni, I don’t want it. 500/5!

Haidilao Hotpot

Address: 138-23 39th Ave, Queens, NY 11354

Have you ever had Chinese Hotpot? Nevermind. Come to Flushing, NY for an awesome experience. Bring your friends and family for a meal where you literally boil meats and vegetables in a boiling broth. 6669/10!

White Bear

Address: 135-02 Roosevelt Ave, Queens, NY 11354

Love Spicy? Love wontons? Then you’d better come on over to White Bear in Flushing, Q ueens to get you the best tasting affordable meal! Have a taste of China without leaving America!

Akino

Address: 82-80, Broadway, NY 11373

Love sushi? Love all you can eats? Say no more! You have to go to Akino located in Elmhurst, NY. This spot will satisfy all of your sushi and Japanese food cravings without sacrificing quality 1000000/5!!! Must go

Sunday in Brooklyn

Address: 348 Wythe Ave Brooklyn, NY 11249

These pancakes are to die for! These are the Sunday Pancakes from Sunday in Brooklyn. If you don’t brunch in New York City, did you even come here? The topping is a hazelnut malt mirror glaze with a brown butter sauce! Additionally, they have amazing cocktails!
